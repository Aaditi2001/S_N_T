Reheating Furnace

Rolling Mill Gearbox

High-performance gearboxes designed to handle heavy loads and ensure smooth transmission in rolling mills.

Mill Stands

Rigid and durable structures that house the rolls and apply pressure to shape the steel.

Shears

Precision cutting equipment used to trim or divide steel products during and after rolling.

Pinch Rolls

Used to guide, grip, and feed hot rolled material accurately between mill stands or to the next processing stage.

High-speed, precision rolling mill specially designed for producing wire rods and TMT bars with excellent finish and strength.
